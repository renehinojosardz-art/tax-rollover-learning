# Mission focused on M&A rollovers

Rene clarified that the learning goal is not general entity-tax literacy, but practical federal tax-structure fluency for tax-free rollovers and equity contributions in M&A deals. Future lessons should support diligent memo drafting around LLC member contributions, partnership interests, real-estate and property contributions, C-corp recapitalizations, Type F reorganizations, founder rollovers, and fund structures.
